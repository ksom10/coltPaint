Who We Are

McCracken Painting:
Your Local Painting Experts

Established over 25 years ago, McCracken Painting has grown to become a pillar in the West Lafayette community, known for our dedication to quality and exceptional service. We are a family-run business that prides itself on using only the highest quality materials, employing the most dedicated painters, and focusing on customer satisfaction to transform your spaces. Whether you need a simple touch-up or a complete makeover, we bring craftsmanship and precision to every project.

Our commitment extends beyond just aesthetics; we understand the importance of timely completion, staying within budget, and providing a hassle-free experience. Our team works closely with each client to ensure their vision is realized, and their expectations are not only met but exceeded.
